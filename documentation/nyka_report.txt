Nykaa Product Data Analysis

Dataset Overview:
The dataset contains 625 products from Nykaa, covering details like Product Name, Brand, Category, Price, Rating, Reviews Count, and more. Some columns had missing or inconsistent data, which required cleaning.

Data Cleaning Steps:

Converted numeric fields (Product Price, Product Rating) to proper numeric types.

Filled missing values for Product Category, Product Url, Product Tags, and Product Contents with placeholders like Unknown or Not Provided.

Replaced missing numeric values with medians (for Price and Rating) or 0 (for Reviews Count).

Removed duplicate entries based on Product Id.

Analysis Highlights:

Identified top brands and categories by number of products, average price, ratings, and reviews.

Found the most expensive, least expensive, highest-rated, and lowest-rated products.

Visualizations highlight key trends in pricing, popularity, and customer feedback across brands and categories.

Key Insights:

Some premium brands like DIOR, CADIVEU, and Burberry have the highest average prices and ratings.

Brands like SUGAR and Nykaa Naturals have the most reviews, indicating strong customer engagement.

Categories like Hair Care and Fragrance dominate in average price, while Skin and Natural products show high ratings and reviews.
